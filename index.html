Dr. Alexis P. Sage DVM

Mobile Equine and Housecall Service

Charlotte, NC - 704-231-8993

Providing professional veterinary care in the comfort of your home.

Mobile veterinary services offer stress-free accomodation for both pets and their owners, as well as flexible appointments for busy schedules .

Alexis Sage, DVM, provides on-site care for horses, dogs and cats in the region surrounding Charlotte, North Carolina. The practice serves Mecklenburg, Union, Stanly and Anson Counties and adjoining areas of South Carolina.

Hospitalization and surgical procedures beyond the scope of a mobile practice are referred to facilities with which Dr. Sage has an established relationship.
