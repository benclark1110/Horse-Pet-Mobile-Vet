Dr. Alexis Sage has over 30 years of experience in professionally treating horses, dogs and cats. She is a graduate of Penn State University and the University of Florida Veterinary School and has participated in prestigious research programs in equine reproduction in both Maryland and Florida. She is a member of the American Association of Equine Practitioners, American Veterinary Medical Association, and the North Carolina Veterinary Medical Association.

Dr. Sage practiced as a Regulatory Veterinarian at Delaware Park Racetrack for three years before owning her own, successful equine and small animal practice in Dover, Delaware. After relocating to Charlotte in 2000 with her family, Dr. Sage opened Mobile Equine and Housecall Service in the greater Charlotte and surrounding areas to provide professional and convenient veterinary care to pet-owners across North Carolina.
